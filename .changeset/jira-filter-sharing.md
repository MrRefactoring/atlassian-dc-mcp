Add Jira filter-sharing tools: `jira_get_filter_share_permissions`, `jira_get_filter_share_permission`, `jira_add_filter_share_permission` (share a saved filter with a group, project, project role, all logged-in users, or globally), and `jira_delete_filter_share_permission`, plus `jira_get_default_share_scope` / `jira_set_default_share_scope` for the current user's default share scope for new filters and dashboards.
